Healert is a mobile application designed to contact Emergency Services without having to call 911

Coming soon to both iOS & Android

Healert is a both a citizen app and a first responder application used to help connect citizens with first responders for faster 911 responses. While our goal is not to eliminate dispatchers, we are creating another option to call 911 while using an app instead. This app will connect users to first responders. Users will open the app, see a series of easy-to-follow prompts, quickly identifying what the emergency is based on the selections the users choose.

Once the users start a call through the app, the app is automatically finding the closest available first responders in the area. The first responders via their Healert tablet app can see in real time what the user is selecting and also information about the users (i.e., medical hx, allergies, prior 911 calls, etc.). While the user is selecting information about the call, they can also see where the first responders are and how long their ETA is.

Why is this needed?

911 is outdated in terms of technology now adays.

In lots of busy cities that are overwhelmed with 911 calls, sometimes your 911 call can be delayed significantly or even not get through where you would have to try again to call. This would relieve some of the high call volume 911 centers face while giving another option for citizens to contact 911.
